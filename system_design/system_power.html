System Power

The following elements need to be powered:

  1. Antennas

    1. Voltage: +9 V and -9 V

    2. Power: 13 mA average, 20 mA peak per antenna. Clarify with John if this is 20 mA for each power rail or total.

  2. Digital board

    1. ADC:

      1. +5 V

        • 1.8 V for the analog section.

        • 1.8 V for the digital section.

    2. FPGA:

      1. 3.3 V is used by the FPGA and the flash memory that carries the FPGA code.

      2. 1.8 V for IO connection to ADC

      3. 1.2 V locally generated from 1.8 on iCEBreaker board. Maybe DCDC?

Suggested Power System

Main power is provided by a 6 cell Lithium Polymer battery with a nominal voltage of 22.2 V. It needs to be checked if a 5 cell is a better alternative. The 6 cell may be generated by smaller units like 2x 3-cell as 6 cell LiPos tend to be very large and heavy.

  1. Maximum voltage: 25.2 V

  2. Minimum voltage: 18 V

Use buck DCDC converters to generate:

  1. 5 V analog. DCDC to 5.5 V filtered and followed by LDO.

  2. 1.8 V analog. DCDC to 2.5 V filtered and followed by LDO.

  3. 1.2 V digital DCDC filtered.

  4. 3.3 V digital, DCDC filtered.
